What is APISA?

Homestead's APISA (Asian Pacific Islander Student Association) creates a safe space for Asian and Pacific Islander students to share their culture and learn about other customs. We'll host events throughout the year, including club gatherings and fundraisers.

How do I join?

To join APISA, you just need to attend meetings. There's no fee.
